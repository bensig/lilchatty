Welcome to Lil' Chatty Setup!

Your friendly, local AI assistant setup.

Get started with your own private ChatGPT-like experience running right on your computer. Lil' Chatty helps you set up Ollama and Open WebUI easily.

Quick Start (MacOS)

Open your Terminal app and paste the following command:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bensig/lilchatty/main/run.sh | bash

This command downloads and runs the setup script, which prepares and launches a local web wizard to guide you through the rest of the process.
